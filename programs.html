03 — STREET CHILDREN Est. 2024

Resilient Street

Sambhawna Schools

Building non-traditional schools for street children — teaching democratic values, foundational literacy, professional skills, and entrepreneurship. A 6–8 year curriculum for ages 4–18.

YEAR 1

Street outreach, trust-building, hygiene support, and facilitating transition into schools and rehabilitation centres.

YEAR 2

Structured work with Child Protection Departments — documentation, school admission, and monitoring in safe environments.

YEAR 3–4

Scale Year 2 work by 2×. Pilot the Sambhawna School curriculum in care homes and schools.

YEAR 5+

Launch Sambhawna Schools and focus on long-term sustainability.
