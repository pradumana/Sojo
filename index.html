About Us

Who We Are

A passionate team building India's most inclusive career ecosystem

Our Mission

To evolve from a community-based job board into a comprehensive career platform in the next five years — serving every job seeker with dignity and opportunity.

Our Vision

A country free of any form of child labour and child beggars — having opportunity to work, while building India into an inclusive and equitable space for job seekers with no employment malpractices.

Our Programs

What We Do

Three powerful initiatives driving change across employment, freelancing, and child welfare

SoJo

Social Jobs Platform

Helping youth and professionals with resume writing, interview prep, and career counseling. We collaborate with nonprofits for recruitment and run active WhatsApp & Telegram communities.

  • Resume Writing & Interview Prep
  • Career Counseling
  • Nonprofit Recruitment Partnerships
  • 1,500+ Active Job Seekers Community
  • 80 Students Placed @ 4 LPA CTC
Join SoJo Community
Active

SoLo

Social Freelancer Platform

Helping young, passionate professionals become freelancers in the social sector. We provide mentorship, training, and free gigs through the SoLo App — building a community of Socialancers.

  • Freelance Mentorship & Training
  • Free Gigs via SoLo App
  • 250-Member WhatsApp Community
  • 60+ Gigs Shared (Proposals, Presentations)
  • Collaboration with Established Socialancers

Resilient Street

Sambhawna Schools

Building Sambhawna Schools for street children — a non-traditional 6-8 year curriculum for ages 4-18 combining FLN, democratic values, and professional/freelancing skills.

Yr 1

Street outreach, hygiene education, 10% kids into schools/rehab centers

Yr 2

Child Protection Dept collaboration, documentation & school admission

Yr 3-4

Scale 2X, test Sambhawna curriculum in care homes

Yr 5+

Launch Sambhawna Schools & sustainability efforts
