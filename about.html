About Us

Purpose-Driven,
Integrity-Led

SoJo Foundation is a non-profit company registered under Section 8 of the Companies Act 2013, with the intent to transform India's work ecosystem.

We are building an India where every adult — regardless of background — has a fair opportunity to earn for themselves, free from discrimination and employment malpractices.

From a community-based job board, we are evolving into a comprehensive career platform supporting youth and professionals in finding fair work and building meaningful, self-determined careers.

🌅

Vision

A country where every adult has the opportunity to earn for themselves — free from employment malpractices and discrimination.

🧭

Mission

To evolve from a community-based job board into a comprehensive career platform in five years, supporting young people in accessing fair work without facing discrimination or malpractice.

💎
